IDM 241 Final Description

Original interface

Trigger

  • Click menu item (about, resume, or contact).

Rules

  • On click, a line appears underneath the item from left to right.
  • On click, the menu background color changes.
  • After 1 second delay, new page loads.

Feedback

  • On click, a line appears underneath the item from left to right.
  • On click, the menu background color changes.
  • After 1 second delay, new page loads.

Loops/modes

  • Mode: If page is active, the menu item is underlined and the associated background color is visible.
